About this project

We are not just translating English names into Chinese, but helping every individual find a name that can fit in the Chinese world. It's a role positioning and a narrative.

The interface combines computational linguistics, semantic analysis, stroke structure, and design aesthetics, so that the names can be accepted by the system and also by the heart.

Conditions for naming
